This guide walks you through the fastest path from sign-up to your first live sale. Quickshops is built around the idea that you should start from something real, not a blank page — so you'll deploy a ready-made store and pick a theme before making any changes.

<Note>
  Your store goes live in under 60 seconds after you click **Make this mine**. No configuration required before that step.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [quickshops.app](https://quickshops.app) and sign up with your email address. You'll receive a magic link to your inbox — click it to log in. No password needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a theme">
    After logging in, you'll see theme previews for your store. Pick the look that fits your brand — light, dark, minimal, or bold. You can switch themes later from **Settings → Appearance**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy your store">
    Click **Make this mine**. Quickshops instantly creates your live store with:

    - Real products and pricing
    - A landing page with copy
    - A working checkout
    - A delivery system for your buyers
    - A subdomain for your store

    <Tip>
      Your store includes realistic starter content. Replace it with your own products using the AI chat editor after deployment.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Stripe">
    After deployment, your dashboard will prompt you to connect a Stripe account so you can receive payments. Click **Connect Stripe** and follow the OAuth flow. If you don't have a Stripe account yet, you can create one during this step.

    <Warning>
      Your store is live and shareable immediately after deploy, but customers cannot complete a purchase until you connect Stripe.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize with the AI editor">
    Once Stripe is connected, open the AI chat editor from your dashboard. The editor starts with suggestions like:

    - Replace products
    - Change pricing
    - Edit your landing page copy
    - Add a new product

    Type what you want to change or tap a suggestion. Each change shows a preview before it applies, and you can undo any action.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share your store and make your first sale">
    When you're ready, copy your store URL from the dashboard and share it. Your buyers will land on your storefront, browse your products, and check out through Stripe. After a successful payment, Quickshops delivers the product to them automatically.

    <Tip>
      Your store URL uses a Quickshops subdomain by default. You can connect a custom domain from **Settings → Domains**.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
