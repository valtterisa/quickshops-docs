This guide walks you through the fastest path from sign-up to your first live sale. Quickshops is built around the idea that you should start from something real, not a blank page, so you'll pick a ready-made template and deploy it instantly before making any changes.

<Note>
  Your store goes live in under 60 seconds after you click **Make this mine**. No configuration required before that step.
</Note>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [quickshops.dev](https://quickshops.dev) and sign up with your email address. You'll receive a magic link to your inbox — click it to log in. No password needed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a template">
    After logging in, you'll see the template gallery. Three templates are available to match common digital product businesses:

    - **Notion Template Store** — sell 2–3 Notion templates with pricing, bundles, and a landing page.
    - **Mini Course Funnel** — sell an online course with pricing tiers, upsells, and an email flow.
    - **Digital Downloads Pack** — sell multiple small digital files with bundle discounts and a simple storefront.

    Browse the templates and click the one that best matches what you want to sell.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy your store">
    On the template preview page, click **Make this mine**. Quickshops instantly creates your live store with:

    - Real products and pricing
    - A landing page with copy
    - A working checkout
    - A delivery system for your buyers
    - A subdomain for your store

    <Tip>
      All templates include realistic product copy and pricing. You can replace the content with your own products using the AI chat editor after deployment.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Stripe">
    After deployment, your dashboard will prompt you to connect a Stripe account so you can receive payments. Click **Connect Stripe** and follow the OAuth flow. If you don't have a Stripe account yet, you can create one during this step.

    <Warning>
      Your store is live and shareable immediately after deploy, but customers cannot complete a purchase until you connect Stripe.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize with the AI editor">
    Once Stripe is connected, open the AI chat editor from your dashboard. The editor starts with suggestions like:

    - Replace products
    - Change pricing
    - Edit your landing page copy
    - Add a new product

    Type what you want to change or tap a suggestion. Each change shows a preview before it applies, and you can undo any action.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Share your store and make your first sale">
    When you're ready, copy your store URL from the dashboard and share it. Your buyers will land on your storefront, browse your products, and check out through Stripe. After a successful payment, Quickshops delivers the product to them automatically.

    <Tip>
      Your store URL uses a Quickshops subdomain by default. You can connect a custom domain from **Settings → Domains**.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
</Steps>
