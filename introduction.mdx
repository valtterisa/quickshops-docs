Quickshops gives you everything you need to turn a digital product idea into a live, selling store in under a minute. Start from a professionally designed template, customize your store through an AI chat editor, and accept payments through Stripe — all without touching a line of code. If you want to build a fully custom storefront, the Headless API and JavaScript SDK give you complete control.

## What you can do with Quickshops

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="One-click deploy" icon="rocket">
    Pick a template and click **Make this mine**. Your store, products, checkout links, and delivery system go live instantly — in under 60 seconds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI chat editor" icon="message-square">
    Customize your store by chatting. Change pricing, swap products, edit your landing page copy, and add new products — just describe what you want.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stripe payments" icon="credit-card">
    Connect your Stripe account to accept one-time payments and recurring subscriptions. Quickshops handles checkout and delivery automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Headless API" icon="code">
    Build a fully custom storefront with any framework. The REST API and JavaScript SDK give you typed access to your store, products, cart, and checkout.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who Quickshops is for

Quickshops works for anyone selling digital goods: creators selling Notion templates, ebooks, or design assets; educators running mini-courses; developers who want a headless commerce backend without building one from scratch.
