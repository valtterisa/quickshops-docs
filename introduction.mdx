Quickshops lets you launch a store, accept payments through Stripe, and deliver digital products in under a minute. Pick a template, customize it with the AI chat editor, and share your checkout link.

## What you can do

- **Deploy in seconds** — Pick a template and click **Make this mine**. Your store, products, and checkout go live instantly.
- **Customize by chatting** — Change pricing, swap products, and edit copy through the AI editor.
- **Accept payments** — Connect Stripe for one-time purchases and subscriptions.
- **Build headless** — Use the REST API and JavaScript SDK for a fully custom storefront.

## Who it's for

Creators selling templates, ebooks, or design assets. Educators running mini-courses. Developers who want a commerce backend without building one from scratch.
