Your Quickshops store is your public-facing storefront where customers discover, browse, and purchase your digital products and subscriptions. Every store gets its own URL the moment you deploy, so you can start sharing and selling within seconds of getting started. You manage everything — from products to payouts — through a single dashboard without touching any code.

## Your store URL

Every store is instantly reachable at a subdomain based on your store slug:

```
yourstore.quickshops.app
```

You can share this link right away. When you are ready, you can connect your own custom domain so your store lives at an address you own — for example `shop.yourbrand.com`. See [Domains](/store/domains) for the full setup steps.

## Customizing your store

You do not need a designer or developer to change how your store looks and what it says. The built-in AI chat editor lets you describe the change you want in plain language and shows you a preview before anything goes live. Whether you want to update your headline, change a price, or add a new product, you just tell it what to do.

<Tip>
  Not sure what to change first? The chat editor always shows guided suggestions so you never have to start from a blank prompt.
</Tip>

## Starting from a template

The fastest way to get a store live is to pick one of the ready-made templates. Each template includes products, pages, pricing, and delivery — a complete working store you can make your own in one click. See [Templates](/store/templates) to browse your options.
