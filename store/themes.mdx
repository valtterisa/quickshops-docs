Every Quickshops store starts from the same storefront layout — products, pricing, pages, checkout links, and automatic delivery are all included from the start. What changes is the **theme**: a preset look and feel you can apply in one click, then refine with the AI chat editor.

## Available themes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Light" icon="sun">
    A clean, bright layout with generous whitespace. Works well for creators, educators, and anyone who wants a friendly, approachable storefront.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dark" icon="moon">
    A high-contrast dark theme that puts your products front and center. Ideal for design assets, dev tools, and premium digital products.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Minimal" icon="grid-2">
    A calm, typography-focused layout with subtle color. Great for small catalogs, resource bundles, and focused product ranges.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bold" icon="sparkles">
    Strong colors and clear section hierarchy for conversion-focused stores. Use this when you want a more energetic, sales-driven feel.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What every store includes

Regardless of theme, your store ships fully loaded so you can start selling immediately:

- **Products** — sample products with descriptions, images, and prices already in place
- **Pages** — a complete storefront landing page ready to share
- **Pricing** — pre-configured prices you can adjust to match what you charge
- **Checkout** — working checkout links attached to each product
- **Delivery** — automatic file or content delivery set up for buyers

<Note>
  All starter content is placeholder. Once your store is deployed, use the AI chat editor to replace product names, descriptions, prices, and images with your own.
</Note>

## How to deploy your store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a theme">
    After signing up, choose the theme that best matches your brand. You can preview how each one looks before you deploy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy your store">
    Click **Make this mine**. Quickshops instantly creates your live store with products, checkout, delivery, and a subdomain — usually in under 60 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make it yours">
    Your dashboard opens with a prompt: "Your store is ready. What do you want to change first?" Use the AI chat editor to swap in your real products, prices, and copy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Changing your theme later

You can switch themes anytime from **Settings → Appearance** in your dashboard. Switching updates colors, fonts, and layout styling — your products, pricing, and content stay the same.

<Tip>
  Prefer fine-grained control? Ask the AI chat editor to adjust individual colors, fonts, or section styles without switching the whole theme.
</Tip>
