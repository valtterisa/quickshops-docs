The AI chat editor is how you shape your Quickshops store after deploying a template. Instead of navigating menus or editing forms, you describe what you want to change in plain language and the editor figures out what to update. You see a preview of every change before it is applied, so nothing goes live until you approve it.

## What you can customize

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Products" icon="box">
    Add new products, update descriptions, change file attachments, or remove products you no longer want to sell.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pricing" icon="tag">
    Change the price of any product, add a discount, or set up a bundle — just tell the editor what you want.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Copy and text" icon="pen">
    Update your homepage headline, product descriptions, section titles, or any other text on your store.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Images" icon="image">
    Replace product images or hero visuals by describing the change or uploading a new file.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Theme" icon="palette">
    Adjust colors, fonts, and the overall look of your storefront to match your brand.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to make a change

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the chat editor">
    From your dashboard, open the AI chat editor. It is always accessible from the main navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Describe what you want">
    Type your request in plain language. You do not need to use any special commands or syntax.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the preview">
    The editor shows you exactly what will change before applying anything. Review the preview and confirm when you are happy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Changes go live">
    Once you confirm, the update is applied to your live store immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Example requests

Here are the kinds of things you can say to the chat editor:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Products">
    - "Add a new product called Notion Dashboard Kit priced at $19"
    - "Remove the starter pack from my store"
    - "Update the description of my course to focus on beginners"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pricing">
    - "Change the price of my ebook to $29"
    - "Set the bundle price to $49"
    - "Make the intro course free"
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Copy and theme">
    - "Update the homepage headline to 'Templates that save you hours'"
    - "Change the primary color to a dark navy"
    - "Make the font look more minimal and modern"
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Guided suggestions

You never have to start from a blank prompt. The chat editor always shows a set of suggested actions based on what is already in your store. Common suggestions include things like replacing a product, changing pricing, or editing a page section.

<Tip>
  If you are not sure what to change, browse the suggestions. They are tailored to your current store and highlight the most common next steps for new sellers.
</Tip>

## Safety and previews

Every change goes through a confirmation step before it affects your live store. You can review exactly what will be different, and if something does not look right you can adjust your request or discard it entirely.

<Note>
  Certain actions — like deleting a product — will ask for an extra confirmation before proceeding.
</Note>
