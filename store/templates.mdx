Quickshops templates give you a fully working store without any setup. Each template ships with real products, configured pricing, sales pages, checkout links, and automatic file delivery — so you start from something live and sellable, not a blank slate. Pick the one that fits what you are selling, click to make it yours, and your store is online within seconds.

## What every template includes

All templates come fully loaded so you can start selling immediately:

- **Products** — sample products with descriptions, images, and prices already in place
- **Pages** — a complete storefront landing page ready to share
- **Pricing** — pre-configured prices you can adjust to match what you charge
- **Checkout** — working checkout links attached to each product
- **Delivery** — automatic file or content delivery set up for buyers

<Note>
  All content in a template is placeholder content. Once your store is deployed, use the AI chat editor to replace product names, descriptions, prices, and images with your own.
</Note>

## How to deploy a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse the templates">
    Go to the Templates page and preview each option. The previews show the real storefront your customers will see.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your template">
    When you find the one that fits, click **Use this template**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your store goes live">
    Quickshops instantly creates your store, products, checkout links, and delivery setup. The whole process takes less than 60 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make it yours">
    Your dashboard opens with a prompt: "Your store is ready. What do you want to change first?" Use the AI chat editor to swap in your real products, prices, and copy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can switch templates later from **Settings → Appearance** in your dashboard if you want to try a different style.
</Tip>
