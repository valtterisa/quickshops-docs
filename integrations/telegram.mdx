Quickshops can connect to a Telegram bot so you get notified the moment a sale comes in — right inside Telegram. Once connected, you can also ask the bot questions about your orders and revenue without opening your dashboard.

## What you need

You need a Telegram account. If you don't have one yet, download the Telegram app and create a free account at [telegram.org](https://telegram.org).

## Connect Telegram to your store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Chat or Settings page">
    In your Quickshops dashboard, navigate to **Chat** in the sidebar, or go to **Settings → Telegram**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect Telegram">
    Select **Connect Telegram**. Quickshops will generate a unique link for your store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the link in Telegram">
    Click the generated link. It will open Telegram and start a conversation with the Quickshops bot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your store is connected">
    Follow the prompt in the Telegram chat to complete the connection. From this point on, the bot is linked to your store.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Only one Telegram account can be connected to a store at a time. If you want to switch to a different Telegram account, disconnect the existing one first from **Settings → Telegram**.
</Note>

## What you can do with the bot

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Instant sale alerts" icon="bell">
    Get a Telegram message every time a customer completes a purchase in your store.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Order queries" icon="search">
    Ask the bot about recent orders, a specific customer, or a product's sales history.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Daily summaries" icon="chart-bar">
    Receive a daily revenue summary so you always have a pulse on how your store is performing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subscription events" icon="rotate-ccw">
    Get notified when a subscription is created, renewed, or cancelled.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Keep your Telegram notifications on for the Quickshops bot so you don't miss time-sensitive alerts, especially for high-value product launches.
</Tip>

## Disconnect Telegram

To unlink your Telegram account, go to **Settings → Telegram** in your dashboard and click **Disconnect**. You can reconnect at any time by following the steps above.
