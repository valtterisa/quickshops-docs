Quickshops integrates with the tools you already use. Connect Stripe to accept payments, Telegram for store notifications, and webhooks to sync data with your own systems.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Stripe" icon="credit-card" href="/integrations/payments">
    Accept one-time payments and recurring subscriptions. Connect your Stripe account in a few clicks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Telegram" icon="message-circle" href="/integrations/telegram">
    Get order notifications and manage your store from Telegram.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Webhooks" icon="webhook" href="/integrations/webhooks">
    Send real-time events to your backend when orders complete, subscriptions change, or products update.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## What you need first

Before setting up integrations, you need an active Quickshops store. If you haven't deployed one yet, start with the [Quickstart](/quickstart) guide.
